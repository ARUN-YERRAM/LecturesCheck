**Summary:**

To achieve top performance as a software engineer at Google, the author recommends seven techniques:

1. **Put in extra work:** Exceed expectations by dedicating more time and effort.
2. **Leverage strengths:** Identify and excel at skills that contribute to your value.
3. **Build a close relationship with your manager:** Ensure that your manager is aware of your accomplishments and goals.
4. **Promote visibility:** Highlight your work and accomplishments to gain recognition from others.
5. **Address weaknesses:** Acknowledge and improve areas where you may fall short.
6. **Take initiative:** Seek out opportunities to showcase your abilities and contribute to the team.
7. **Recognize the role of luck:** While effort and execution are crucial, acknowledge that external factors can also influence performance.
