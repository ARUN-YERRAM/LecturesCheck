**Summary:**

The YouTuber, who runs Algo Expert, explains his 5-month hiatus from YouTube. He reveals that Algo Expert's new infrastructure engineer interview prep product, Info Expert, is now available. Additionally, a second new product will be released in a month.

However, he will prioritize creating content that he genuinely wants to share rather than feeling obligated to post.
